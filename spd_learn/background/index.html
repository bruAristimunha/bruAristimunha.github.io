Background#

This section provides a comprehensive introduction to Symmetric Positive Definite (SPD) geometry and its applications in deep learning. Understanding these fundamental concepts will help you make the most of the SPD Learn library.

At a high level, SPD matrices offer a rich geometric structure that captures second-order statistics and correlations in data. By working directly in the manifold of SPD matrices rather than treating them as elements of Euclidean space, we can leverage their inherent geometric properties to build more effective machine learning models.

The following tutorials detail the key concepts, computational approaches, and deep learning integration strategies for working with SPD matrices.
